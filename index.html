What Is VPR


VPR is a Fictional Military-esque group that started on Recroom as 'VAF' to mock Rec-Military and SWAT-Military for their overly serious nature, When VPR was formed the map was among the worst of the maps when compared to late recroom era maps. (more in timeline section)


VPR follows the leadership of 'High Command' during raids, wars, and events to ensure sucess, at the head of High Command sits the ranks 1IC, 2IC, and 3IC. (more on ranks in the ranks section)





Timeline Of VPR


VPR originally started as VAF, under the command of C-13 it slowly grew, little progress had been made until the 'very small update' which had introduced a massive military base map, it included an airfield, a Hicom building, a nuke sequence, an army barracks, and multiple more things.


The original game for VAF hit 1,000 visits during the 'hidden base' update that turned the base from a military base into a secret underground mountain base. During that time, VAF renamed itself into VPR, marking the now more serious evolution that remains made specifically for fun.


Disaster, however had struck almost a week after the 'Vartov Update' when Recroom announced itself to be shutting down, many VPR allies had instantly ceased to exist, VPR itself held steady, tried PS3D, Tried Roblox, and even made a seperate divison for No Mans Sky. Currently it remains without a steady game however its allies that survived are reccomending games, such as RecPlace which is becoming ever more likely of an idea.
